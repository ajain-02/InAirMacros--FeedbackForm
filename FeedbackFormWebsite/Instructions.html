Instructions

Unzip all of the zip file's contents as it is in the folder, and run the program named MI3-FacialNavigation.exe

Here are some of the speech commands that are available when in speech modes. When not in a speech mode in this micro-build you will be in facial navigation mode (table below). It is currently English only but future versions will be multilingual.

Saying the "Next Mode" command will rotate through these options:

  1. Nose Navigation with Speech commands
  2. Nose Navigation with Facial Gestures
  3. Eye Tracking with Speech commands
  4. Eye Tracking with Facial Gestures
  1. Whenever you enter eye gaze you will run through calibration, which has onscreen instructions.
  2. Say "click", "double click" and "right click" for those mouse buttons.
  3. In any editable text field, including in Word, Outlook, Teams etc; say "transcribe". Speak with short English sentences and pause, and it should appear. To end transcribe mode, say "transcribe" again to turn it off.
  4. In any browser or office app, "page up" and "page down".
  5. In powerpoint you can say "next", "previous", "show fullscreen" and "escape".
  6. "windows run" will bring up the windowskey+R dialog box.
  7. If you are in nose mode, say "butterfly" when you move your head, to move the area of interest box to where your head is at that point. (still in testing).

  • The facial gestures in non-speech modes are:

Nose Eyes
Smile on/off navigation
Fish Face left click left click
Eyebrow Raised double click double click
Mouth Open right click right click
